Bridgeport

Where simplicity, capability and honesty meet private capital:

We aim to maximize long-term return on invested capital (ROIC) & free cash flow (FCF).

We have a Long-term focus.

We aim to be permanent owners and have permanent partners.

We aim to have like-minded partners.

We favor enterprise that is anchored to psychological permanence or that is critical in nature, and avoid exposure to consumer fads.

We shamelessly learn from and embrace the business and investment principles of: John D. Rockefeller, Benjamin Graham, Hamilton Helmer, Warren Buffett, Charlie Munger and Clayton Christensen.

We prefer to purchase at or near cyclical lows within long-term growth cycles.

We favor simplicity, quality and good incentives structures.

We value efficiency and thrift and have the unique ability to both not act for long periods of time when opportunity is not obvious and act decisively when it is; this is easier said than done.

We favor companies that generate high returns with little to no use of debt or leverage.

We favor companies that show significant ability to generate free cash flow and economic returns on investment in both the present and the future.

We favor companies that show the ability to execute operations effectively and efficiently, while embracing "Blue Ocean Strategy." Ref.

We favor companies that have high-integrity management that are aligned with owners'.

Finally, we seek to make purchases at sensible prices that allow these characteristics to accrue to our basis.

We commit to lifelong refinement, adaptation and learning; however, we do not waver on our principles.

Sincerely,

Scott

Bridgeport Asset Management, LLC was incorporated in Washington State in 2018.
